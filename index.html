How to Play Backgammon

Move all checkers home and bear them off first.
Roll dice and move checkers based on values.
Capture opponent pieces by landing alone on them.
If all checkers are home, you can bear them off.
First player to remove all checkers wins.

Press [M] to return to the Main Menu.
