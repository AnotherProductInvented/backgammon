How to Play Backgammon

Black moves from point 1 to point 24. White moves in reverse from point 24 to point 1. When a checker is hit, it goes to the bar and must re‑enter into the opponent’s home board. White re‑enters in points 19–24 and bears off from points 1–6. Black re‑enters in points 1–6 and bears off from points 19–24.
