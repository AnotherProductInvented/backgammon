How to Play Backgammon

Move all your checkers into your home board and bear them off before your opponent does.
Roll two dice; each die represents a move value. Doubles give four moves.
Click a checker to see its legal moves.
If a checker lands on a single opponent checker, it is hit and removed.
The first player to remove all their checkers wins.
